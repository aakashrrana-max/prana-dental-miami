## Colors
- White bg: #FFFFFF
- Grey light bg: #F2F2F2
- Grey mid: #E4E4E4
- Grey sand (dividers only): #C8C3BB
- Charcoal text: #1A1A1A
- Text muted: #6A6A6A
- Black (buttons): #000000
- Miami terracotta (eyebrows/accents ONLY): #C4845C
- Border: #DEDEDE
- WhatsApp: #25D366
- NO beige/ivory (#FAF8F5, #F2EFE9, #FDFCFA)
- NO gold (#C9A96E, #B8966E)

## Typography
- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond 300/400 (serif, editorial, never bold)
- Body/UI: DM Sans 400/500/600
- Hero: 72/48px, section: 52/36px, body: 16px, buttons: 13px uppercase
- Eyebrow: 11px uppercase tracking-[0.2em] in miami terracotta
- Italic in headlines: Cormorant Garamond italic weight 300

## Style direction
- Clean white + neutral grey editorial luxury healthcare
- Apple-level smoothness and spacing
- Generous whitespace, calm, premium
- Dark sections ONLY for fear-reduction and final CTA
- No oversized chunky buttons — small elegant CTAs (13px, 14px padding)
- No flashy/template effects, no AI-looking imagery
- Strong visual anchors per section
- Border-radius: 2px max
